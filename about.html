Our Story

A little something

There isn't something magically different about the lives we live or the stories we'll pass down. We're a bunch of college students trying to find that little magic in the little everyday moments we take for granted. And maybe we don't have the most exciting adventurous stories to tell, or have something entirely different from the crowd, All we're trying to do is add a little magic to the mundane.
